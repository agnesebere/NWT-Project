NLNG Post-Primary Scholarship for Nigerian Students, 2018/2019

NLNG Post-Primary Scholarship is currently ongoing for Nigerian Students. The NLNG Post-Primary Scholarship scheme aims at supporting high performing basic six pupils in company’s host communities and the oil-producing states of Nigeria to access post primary education uninterruptedly. Interested pupils should see how to apply below.

The NLNG Post-Primary Scholarship scheme kicked off in 2012 with 28 beneficiaries, and has grown to a total of 172 beneficiaries by 2015.
Prospective pupils are admitted onto the scheme based on excellent performance at the annual National Examinations Council’s (NECO) Common Entrance Examinations.

Applications are invited for the Nigeria LNG Limited scholarship program which is provided to high performing basic six pupils in Nigeria.
